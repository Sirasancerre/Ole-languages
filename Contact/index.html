Olé Languages is situated in the city center of Barcelona within 15 minutes walking distance from Plaça Catalunya. It is located on a picturesque, pedestrian avenue, the Avenida Mistral in the neighbourhood of Sant Antoni that features many small shops, restaurants and little local cafes and bars with outdoorterrazas.
